# Product Brief: Local Signal Scanner

## Summary
Local Signal Scanner is a web-based audit tool for local businesses that want to understand how visible they are across search, maps, reviews, website SEO, and AI-generated answers. The product helps owners, marketers, and agencies quickly identify gaps and prioritize improvements.

## Problem
Local businesses often struggle to answer basic questions about their digital presence:
- Are they showing up in local search and map results?
- Is their website optimized for local intent?
- Are reviews and reputation signals helping or hurting performance?
- How well do they appear in AI-assisted answer experiences?

Most teams rely on fragmented tools and manual checks, which makes it hard to get a clear, actionable view of performance.

## Product Vision
Create a single, guided experience that converts scattered local visibility signals into a clear audit report with practical recommendations.

## Target Users
- Small business owners
- Local marketing agencies
- SEO consultants
- Franchise or multi-location operators

## Core Experience
Users can:
1. Enter a business name, location, or website.
2. Run a local visibility audit.
3. Review a structured report covering listings, SEO, reviews, keywords, and AI-readiness.
4. Export findings or share them with stakeholders.

## MVP Scope
The first release should focus on a lightweight, high-value experience:
- Business profile summary
- Website and SEO signal audit
- Review and reputation snapshot
- Keyword gap and local intent insights
- Recommendation cards with simple next steps
- Exportable report

## Success Metrics
- Users complete a scan and view a report
- Users return for a follow-up audit
- Users act on recommendations by updating listings or content
- Report exports are used in client or internal workflows

## Non-Goals for v1
- Full-scale rank tracking
- Deep Google Business Profile management
- Automated posting to every directory
- Advanced competitor benchmarking beyond the initial audit

## Positioning
Local Signal Scanner is a practical visibility audit platform for local businesses that want a faster way to understand what is working, what is missing, and what to fix next.
